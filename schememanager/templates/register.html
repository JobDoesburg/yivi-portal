Register

To register a new organization, a valid KvK credential of the organization must be disclosed. This can only be done by a KvK registered authorized representative of the organization. If you are not a KvK authorized representative, please contact the authorized representative of your organization.

After initial registration, the authorized representative will be able to give other users access to the organization by specifying their email address.

If your organization is not registered at the KvK or you are not a KvK authorized representative, you should contact Privacy by Design Foundation via email for further assistance.
